Part 1: The Enigmatic Library

In a distant city, the grand library, once a beacon of knowledge and civic pride, now stands in eerie abandonment on the city's outskirts. It holds within its ancient corridors the secrets of the past, awaiting a courageous soul to uncover the mysteries it guards.
Meet Alex, a young and passionate wizard with an insatiable thirst for ancient wisdom. His fascination with the arcane and the enigmatic led him to the library's doorstep. Driven by a profound need to understand its mysteries, he embarks on a quest to breathe life back into the long-forgotten sanctuary.
