Part 10: A Legacy of Wisdom

The final door opens to reveal a treasure trove of ancient knowledge. Alex's quest to restore the library has unveiled its true legacy, once hidden and now recovered. The library, once abandoned and veiled in mystery, is now a beacon of wisdom and discovery for the city. The knowledge Alex rediscovered will be celebrated for generations to come, not just as a legacy of the library but also as part of Alex's own personal journey of self-discovery and enlightenment.
