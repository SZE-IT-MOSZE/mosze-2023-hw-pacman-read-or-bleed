Part 4: The Perilous Ascent

With each step upwards, the challenges grow in complexity and danger. Skeletons of greater power and cunning challenge Alex at every turn. To unearth the third part of the library's story, he must master the treacherous traps and confront the ever-more-formidable foes that bar his way.
At the zenith of the library, Alex enters a vast chamber shrouded in mystique. It is the heart of the library and the dominion of the dreaded Skeleton King. To reach their ultimate confrontation, Alex must unravel the enigmatic puzzles, navigate dark labyrinths, and face a mounting sense of foreboding.
