Part 3: An Ancient Enigma

Beyond the door, an enchanting chamber reveals scrolls and tomes filled with riddles from an ancient age. Alex becomes immersed in deciphering their enigmatic codes. Unraveling the cryptic message unveils not just a key but also the first hints of the library's hidden past.
Each unlocked door whispers tales of the library's tumultuous history. Alex unearths the story of a mighty sorcerer who once called the library home. The sorcerer's tragic fall from grace and transformation into the ominous Skeleton King looms large in the tale of the library's past.
